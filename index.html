Privacy And Safety

The live experience is still designed around educational use, privacy-aware handling, and clear expectations about what the platform does and does not do.

AIcura provides educational information only and does not diagnose medical conditions.
Submitted questions are not stored on the AIcura application server.
Requests are forwarded to the configured AI provider for processing in real time.
Retinal scans and patient details are treated as sensitive medical data and should be handled with extra care.
Users should avoid entering names, addresses, birth dates, or other personal identifiers during demos.
AIcura is a working retinal insight demo with live educational Q&A, retinal fundus validation, and guided screening output. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, or replace licensed healthcare professionals.
