Retinal scans, AI insight, and health education

From a retinal scan to broader health insight.

AIcura is being built as a retinal insight platform. Our long-term direction is to analyze retinal scans with AI to estimate broader health factors, including heart-health indicators and diabetes-related risk patterns. The live feature today is a health question-and-answer assistant for general and retinal-related educational guidance.

AIcura does not store submitted questions on its own server. Because retinal images and patient details are sensitive medical data, the platform is designed around privacy-first handling and users should avoid entering identifying information during demos.

Privacy And Safety

AIcura provides educational information only and does not diagnose medical conditions.
Submitted questions are not stored on the AIcura application server.
Requests are forwarded to the configured AI provider for processing in real time.
Retinal scans and patient details are treated as sensitive medical data and should be handled with extra care.
Users should avoid entering names, addresses, birth dates, or other personal identifiers during demos.
AIcura is being developed for retinal-scan based health insight and educational support. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, or replace licensed healthcare professionals.
